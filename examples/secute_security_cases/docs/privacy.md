# Privacy Policy Fixture

This privacy policy states that personal data and personal information are
processed only to provide the service and support authorized account users. The
service gives notice before processing customer data for a new purpose and uses
consent where required.

The service applies data minimization: analytics queries must use the minimum
necessary fields and must not disclose customer_email or tenant_id unless an
authorized role and release policy allow the result to leave the trusted
boundary.

Users can access, correct, and delete their information. Customer data is
retained only for the documented retention period and deleted after verified
deletion requests or contract termination.
